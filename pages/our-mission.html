BODY

Our highest quality ingredients are non-GMO, organic, vegan, unbleached, and a few are gluten-free: All are not too sweet. One of the catalysts for 3B Healthy is a desire to offer the best ingredients to enliven my aging parents and by extension everyone’s health.

EARTH

What we take from the Earth has to balance what we return in exchange. Our compostable packaging honors creation rather than contributing to the enormous global waste


World Centric provides zero waste solutions to reduce environmental impact. Their containers are made from NatureWorks Ingeo™ PLA compostable plastic, derived from plants grown in the U.S.
  • Suitable for food up to 120° F
  • Freezer safe
  • Not microwavable
  • Composts in 2-4 months in a commercial composting facility, or 4-6 months in home composter. The packages DO NOT go into your recycle bin; they return to the soil
